Janjaruka awareness campaign is a movement formed by youths to create awareness to people about HIV

Janjaruka awareness campaign helps the HIV victims and from needy homes by spreading love to them and sharing items with them,Janjaruka fights for the rights of women and girls who are victims of HIV/AIDS, Janjaruka awareness campaign helps people come together in unity to support Hiv patients

HIV is a disease that does not discriminate. Anyone can contract this disease, which is why it is important for students to be AWARE of how to protect themselves and prevent the transmission of HIV.

Janjaruka hosts events where people can interact and motivate each other. providing a testing location. Take an HIV test, get in care, and stay in care. Make appointments for people. Share their stories to motivate others. Show their support for your mission and for people affected by HIV/AIDS. Watch a video ,listen to songs amongst other fun activities.
